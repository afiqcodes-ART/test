Positions

Technician

Job Descriptions

  1. Conduct repair work with known repair methods at reception time
  2. Conduct overhaul and trouble-shooting of vehicles
  3. Perform air-conditioning repair upon customer complaint
  4. Perform tire changing, tire balancing & wheel alignment job
  5. Co-ordinate with foreman when an additional job is identified
  6. Assist the foreman in technical, housekeeping, and safety on daily operation

Job Requirements

  1. Minimum SKM Certificate Level 3 in Automotive or equivalent. Diploma in Automotive is an added advantage
  2. Minimum 1 year of working experience as a technician in automotive repairs industry
  3. Basic reading and writing skills in English & Malay
  4. Basic computer skills
  5. Valid class D driving license

Service Consultant

Lead your own future in Sales with Honda. Be trained by industry professionals on products and soft skills. Meet and greet prospective customers, invite them for test drives, and close sales.

Job Descriptions

  1. Close sales using consultative approach
  2. Provide impartial advice
  3. Deliver and maintain quality service
  4. Develop product knowledge
  5. Deliver individual and team sales targets
  6. Participate in sales activities, events, and roadshows
  7. Manage delivery process of new cars
  8. Salary: Commission Basis

Job Requirements

  1. Higher Secondary/ STPM/ "A" Level/ Diploma or equivalent
  2. Sales & automotive experience an advantage
  3. Proficient in English & Bahasa Malaysia
  4. Self-motivated, target-driven, passionate
  5. Valid class D driving license & own transport

Cashier

Job Descriptions

  1. Greet customers and handle cash, credit, QR-pay, or online transactions
  2. Ensure invoice pricing is correct
  3. Issue change, receipts, or gate pass
  4. Handle SAP invoice, redeem vouchers, count money, sell merchandise, bag items, maintain checkout cleanliness, keep transaction reports, answer customer questions

Job Requirements

  1. Minimum Diploma in Accounting/Business Administration with CGPA ≥2.80
  2. Good personality and communication skills in Malay & English
  3. Discipline, work ethics, integrity, multitasking, cheerful, energetic, self-motivated, team player
  4. Able to start work immediately

Insurance Admin

Job Descriptions

  1. Open quotation for renewal Honda / Non Honda cars
  2. Assist customer by call & counter
  3. Generate cover note insurance
  4. Process roadtax renewal
  5. Sales for motor insurance, personal target
  6. After service renewal - endorsement, claim etc

Job Requirements

  1. Diploma in Insurance / Business Administration or equivalent
  2. Good command in English and Bahasa Malaysia
  3. Proficient in Microsoft Excel

Facilities Assistant

Job Descriptions

  1. Handle office maintenance and administrative support services
  2. Coordinate maintenance, troubleshooting, checks and controls on electrical and mechanical appliances
  3. Supervise office/building cleanliness and update checklist daily

Job Requirements

  1. Diploma in Air-Conditioning / Electrical / Building Services with 1 year servicing experience (Fresh Grad welcome)
  2. Competent in Microsoft Office
  3. Valid class D driving license
  4. Willing to work long hours or on rest day

IT Executive

Responsible for maintaining the company's IT network, servers, computers, applications, and security systems at both branches.

Key Duties

  1. Maintain server operations, OS updates, backups, and data security
  2. Ensure internet access and WIFI coverage
  3. Maintain availability of IT hardware/software
  4. Handle documentation, contractor arrangement, PC leasing/purchasing
  5. Research new technology enhancement

Foreman

Job Requirements

  1. SKM Level 3 / SPM or higher
